Our Story

Rooted in European tradition, made with heart

Swiss Miss Bakery is a home-based artisan bakery inspired by old-world techniques and simple, quality ingredients. We specialize in naturally leavened sourdough, rustic artisan breads, tender cake loaves, buttery cookies, and custom cakes for special occasions.

Every loaf is slow-fermented for flavour and digestibility, every bake crafted in small batches, and every recipe made to bring a little comfort and nostalgia to your table.

From our kitchen to your home — thank you for supporting local. 🌿

The Art of Sourdough

Every loaf earns its crumb

Sourdough is at the soul of what we do. Each loaf is made with a living starter, long cold fermentation, and careful hand-shaping. The result is bread with real depth of flavour, beautiful texture, and improved digestibility — because slow is always better.

How to Order

We currently take all orders through Instagram DM — no online store yet. Here's how it works:

1

Browse Instagram

Explore our feed at @swissmissbakeryyeg for current offerings, seasonal specials, and bake inspiration.

2

Send a DM

Message us with what you'd like — bread, cake, cookies — along with your preferred pickup date and any details.

3

We'll Confirm

We'll reply with availability and pricing. Custom cake orders require a deposit to hold your date.

4

Pickup & Enjoy

Fresh from our oven to your door. Delivery may be available — just ask when you message us.
